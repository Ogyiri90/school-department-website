Who We Are

The Faculty of Computing and Information Systems (FOCIS) is one of the premier academic faculties dedicated to advancing education and research in the fields of computing, information technology, and information systems.

Established to meet the growing demand for skilled technology professionals, FOCIS offers a range of undergraduate and postgraduate programmes designed to equip students with both theoretical knowledge and practical expertise.

Our graduates go on to excel in roles spanning software engineering, data science, cybersecurity, cloud architecture, IT management, and entrepreneurship across Ghana and the globe.

Our Mission & Vision

Guiding principles that drive everything we do

🎯 Our Mission

To provide quality education and conduct impactful research in computing and information systems that produces skilled, ethical, and innovative graduates ready to solve real-world challenges through technology.

🔭 Our Vision

To be a globally recognised centre of excellence in computing education, research, and innovation — driving digital transformation and producing leaders who shape the future of technology in Africa and beyond.

Faculty Leadership

FOCIS is led by a team of distinguished academics and administrators committed to advancing computing education.

The Dean provides strategic direction for the faculty, overseeing academic programmes, research initiatives, and partnerships with industry and international institutions.

Heads of Department manage the day-to-day operations of each department, ensuring curriculum quality, student welfare, and faculty development.

Together, the leadership team works to maintain FOCIS's reputation as a centre of excellence in computing and information systems.
