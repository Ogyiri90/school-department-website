About FOCIS

The Faculty of Computing and Information Systems (FOCIS) is a leading academic faculty dedicated to producing world-class graduates in computing, information technology, and information systems.

Our programmes blend rigorous theory with practical, hands-on experience — ensuring students are prepared for careers in software development, cybersecurity, cloud computing, data science, and more.

With state-of-the-art laboratories, experienced faculty, and strong industry partnerships, FOCIS provides an environment where innovation thrives.

Why Choose FOCIS?

Industry-Ready Curriculum: Our programmes are regularly updated in consultation with industry partners to ensure graduates have the skills employers demand.

Hands-On Learning: From fully equipped computer labs to real-world project work, students gain practical experience from day one.

Expert Faculty: Learn from experienced academics and industry professionals who bring real-world insight into the classroom.

Research & Innovation: Students have opportunities to participate in cutting-edge research in AI, cybersecurity, cloud computing, and more.
